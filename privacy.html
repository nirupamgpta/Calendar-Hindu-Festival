🛡️ Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 11 July 2025

Thank you for installing the Hindu Calendar & Reminder Chrome Extension. Your privacy is extremely important to us, and we want you to feel safe using this tool.

🔐 What Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any server. All information you enter — such as birthdays, anniversaries, or reminders — is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's local storage. It stays only on your device.

📡 Data Sharing

  • We do not share your data with third-party services.
  • We do not use any cookies or tracking technologies.
  • We do not send your data to any external servers.

🔄 Permissions

The extension may request permission to:

  • Access Chrome local storage — to save your reminders
  • Display notifications — to remind you of saved events

These permissions are used only for providing the features of this extension and nothing else.
